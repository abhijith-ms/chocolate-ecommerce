# Website Development Proposal
## Ali & Company - International Cuisine eCommerce Platform

---

## Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the development of a modern, mobile-first eCommerce website for **Ali & Company**, specializing in authentic international cuisine. The platform will enable customers to browse your menu, manage orders through an intuitive shopping cart, and complete purchases seamlessly via WhatsApp integration.
